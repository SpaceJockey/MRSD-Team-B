--- title: System Level Requirements --- Critical Requirements
  • Maintain firm attachment to the spacecraft surface using a mechanism that requires neither gravity nor atmospheric pressure
  • Traverse the craft at a reasonable speed. Technical Performance Measure: Traversal speed of at least 10 meters per hour
  • Accomplish planar transitions of at least 30 degrees
  • Able to be validated under terrestrial conditions
  • Prototype cost less than $4000
Essential Requirements
  • Perform mapping and localization using stereo cameras and other sensors
  • Total mass less than 10 kilograms
  • Total folded volume of less than 8 standard CubeSats (10 cubic centimeters)
  • Operate without tethers for battery or communications
  • Autonomous path-planning to plot traversal to user-specified waypoints
  • Accomplish planar transitions of at least 60 degrees
  • Maintain attachment in terrestrial conditions on a vertical surface
Desirable Requirements
  • Thermal imaging capability
  • Capable of using a rotary tool to manipulate a fastener on the spacecraft surface
  • Autonomous identification of surface anomalies
  • Space-readiness plan upon project completion
  • Accomplish planar transitions of 90 degrees
  • Maintain attachment in terrestrial conditions on an inverted surface
